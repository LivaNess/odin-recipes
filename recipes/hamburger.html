Hamburger

Description:

A hamburger is a sandwich made from ground meat or vegetable origin, bound together in the form of a steak cooked on the grill or grill, although it can also be fried or baked.

It is presented in a light bread divided in two that has an oval shape. It is usually accompanied by onion rings, lettuce leaves, a slice of tomato, slices of pickles and French fries.

Ingredients:

  • 500 g minced meat
  • 1/2 onion
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 egg
  • Salt, pepper, oregano and oil, to taste
  • 4 small country breads
  • 8 slices of Cheddar cheese
  • 1 purple onion
  • cherry tomatoes, to taste
  • green leaves (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the minced meat, the onion, the garlic and the egg until all the ingredients are well integrated.
  2. Make four meatballs by hand and shape them into patties.
  3. Cook the hamburgers on both sides in an oiled skillet, until they are well cooked and golden brown.
  4. In an oiled pan, brown the onion slices and the tomatoes cut in half.
  5. Heat the buns in the oven, open them in the middle, distribute the hamburgers, add 2 slices of cheddar cheese, a little onion and some tomatoes.
  6. Suggestion: serve the hamburgers accompanied by baked potatoes seasoned with fresh oregano, salt and pepper.
