Lasagna

Description:

Lasagna is a type of pasta. It is often served on overlapping sheets interspersed with layers of ingredients to taste, most often meat in bolognese sauce and bechamel sauce.

Its origin is Italian and the dish that is usually prepared with meat is called baked lasagna.

Ingredients:

  • 400 grams of ground beef (lean and low fat)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons of oil
  • 1/2 white onion finely chopped
  • 1 garlic clove finely chopped
  • 2 cups finely chopped green bell pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of Knorr® Juicy Rib
  • 12 sheets of pasta for precooked lasagna
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 150 grams of grated mozzarella cheese
  • 10 fresh basil leaves

Steps

  1. Heat the oil and fry the chopped onion with the garlic and pepper. Once they change color (from white to transparent) add the meat and cook until browned.
  2. Season with Knorr® Juicy Rib, pepper and cinnamon. Reserve.
  3. In a previously greased baking pan, place a sauce base and a layer of ground meat. Cover with a layer of lasagna sheets.
  4. Add more sauce, more meat and cover again with pasta. Repeat these steps until the mold is filled.
  5. Finish with a layer of tomato sauce. Spread the mozzarella cheese over the lasagna and cover with aluminum foil.
  6. Bake at 180°C for 35 to 50 minutes.
  7. Five minutes before you take the lasagna out of the oven, remove the foil to allow the cheese to brown evenly.
  8. Serve and decorate with fresh basil leaves.
